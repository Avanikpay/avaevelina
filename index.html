STRANGER THINGS (2016-2021)

The nostalgic sci-fi/horror thriller about a missing boy is an homage to the cult favourites of the '80s and has layers upon layers of terrifying twists and turns. Created by Wayward Pines scribes Matt and Ross Duffer and armed with a cast led by Winona Ryder, Stranger Things turned out to be one of the biggest, most binge-worthy hits of the Summer 2016.
